FAQs


How much does it cost to get started?

It’s completely free to chat!

Do you only help with finding full-time jobs?

Riqruit will recommend jobs based on what you say you’re looking for. Whether it be an internship, part-time job, or full time job, Riqruit’s got your back.

Who’s helping me find these jobs?

When contacting Riqruit you are paired with a Riqruiter who has the goal of matching you with a job that meets exactly what you’re looking for. Our Riqruiters have worked with HR groups with various well known companies, and know exactly what hiring managers look for.

What happens when I get a job?

Just txt STOP and we will discontinue sending your personalized job recommendations, but we'll always be one text away if looking for any career advice!
